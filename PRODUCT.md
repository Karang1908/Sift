# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
A small, known team sharing a local network, looking to query and extract structured information from documents. The interface must be straightforward and highly intuitive, even for non-technical users.

## Product Purpose
Sift is a single-screen dashboard utility that allows users to upload documents (PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, images, text), cache the parsed text, query them using AI, stream query results, and export them into customized reports (Word, Excel, PDF, Markdown).

## Positioning
An industrial-grade, single-screen dashboard for AI-driven document parsing, citation-tagging, and multi-format reporting.

## Brand Personality
Minimal, editorial, and typography-focused. Clean, premium, and structured layout that prioritizes content readability and intuitive layout flow.

## Anti-references
- Raw, unstyled terminal-like interfaces.
- Overly technical command-line-styled layouts.
- Generic SaaS clichés, such as purple-to-blue gradients or heavily nested "ghost cards."

## Design Principles
- **Readability First**: Clear typographical hierarchy, elegant type scaling, and ample whitespace to make complex document analysis easily digestible.
- **Immediate Utility**: Single-screen layout that exposes all core features (upload, prompt, response, export) directly without hidden pages or complex navigation.
- **Empower Non-Technical Users**: Clear instructions, sensible defaults, and smart interactive components to simplify document extraction and custom exports.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Simple, clear labels and intuitive form structures tailored for non-technical team members.
- Strong text contrast (WCAG AA alignment) and scalable typography for comfortable readability.
